# Privacy Policy for Torch - Flashlight

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Torch - Flashlight ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Device Sensors
Our app accesses the following device sensors to provide its core functionality:
- **Camera/Flashlight**: To control the LED flashlight on your device
- **Accelerometer**: To detect shake gestures for hands-free flashlight control
- **Magnetometer/Compass**: To provide compass functionality

### Data Storage
- **Preferences**: We store your app settings (brightness, color temperature, shake sensitivity) locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information

## How We Use Information

All sensor data is processed locally on your device and is used solely for:
- Controlling the device flashlight
- Detecting shake gestures
- Displaying compass direction
- Adjusting screen brightness and color temperature

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Collect personal information
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Store any data on external servers

## Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Fonts for typography, which requires an internet connection. Google Fonts may collect standard web request data. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.

## Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:
| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Camera | Access LED flashlight |
| Vibrate | Haptic feedback on button presses |
| Wake Lock | Keep screen on during screen light mode |
| Internet | Load Google Fonts |
| High Sampling Rate Sensors | Accurate shake detection and compass |

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages.
